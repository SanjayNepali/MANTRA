Technical support

Click on "Sign Up" at the top right of the page and choose whether you want to join as a Fan or Celebrity. Fill in your details, verify your email, and you're ready to start exploring MANTRA!

Celebrities can apply for verification by going to their profile settings and clicking "Apply for Verification". You'll need to upload KYC documents including a government-issued ID and proof of your celebrity status. Our team will review your application within 5-7 business days.

We accept multiple payment methods including eSewa, Khalti, major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Navigate to the celebrity's profile and click the "Follow" button. You'll receive updates about their posts, events, and exclusive content. Some celebrities offer premium subscriptions for additional exclusive content.

Fan clubs are communities of fans who share their passion for a celebrity. You can join official fan clubs created by celebrities or community fan clubs created by other fans. Fan clubs offer exclusive content, discussions, and special events.

Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the link in your email to create a new password.

Browse upcoming events on the Events page. Click on an event to see details and purchase tickets if required. You'll receive a confirmation email with event access details. On event day, click "Join Event" from your dashboard or the event page.
